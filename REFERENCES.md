# Academic References

## Core References

### Foundational Work
- **Barnhart, C., Johnson, E., Nemhauser, G., Savelsbergh, M., & Vance, P. (2003).** *Airline Crew Scheduling.* 
  - Comprehensive survey of airline crew scheduling problems and solution methodologies
  
- **Klabjan, D., Johnson, E., Nemhauser, G., Gelman, E., & Ramaswamy, S. (1999).** *Solving Large Airline Crew Scheduling Problems.*
  - Practical implementations and computational results for large-scale problems

### Column Generation Theory
- **Desrosiers, J., & Lübbecke, M. (2005).** *A Primer in Column Generation.*
  - Theoretical foundations and practical aspects of column generation
  
- **Vanderbeck, F., & Wolsey, L. A. (2010).** *Reformulation and Decomposition of Integer Programs.*
  - Mathematical foundations for decomposition techniques

### Set Partitioning & Crew Pairing
- **Vance, P. H. (1993).** *Branch-and-Price Algorithms for the One-Dimensional Cutting Stock Problem.*
  - Early applications of branch-and-price methodology

- **Barnhart, C., Hane, C., & Vance, P. (2000).** *Using Branch-and-Price-and-Cut to Solve Origin-Destination Integer Multicommodity Flow Problems.*
  - Integration of cutting planes with branch-and-price

## Related Applications

### Airline Operations
- Aircraft routing and scheduling
- Flight crew rostering under uncertainty
- Integrated aircraft and crew scheduling
- Dynamic crew scheduling with flight disruptions

### Optimization Methods
- Shortest path algorithms for network design
- Integer programming and LP relaxation theory
- Mixed-integer programming techniques
- Heuristic pricing strategies

## Recommended Reading Order

1. Start with Desrosiers & Lübbecke for column generation fundamentals
2. Read Barnhart et al. (2003) for problem context and real-world constraints
3. Study Klabjan et al. (1999) for implementation details
4. Explore Vanderbeck & Wolsey (2010) for advanced decomposition techniques
